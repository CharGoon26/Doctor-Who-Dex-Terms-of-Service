## 1. Usage Agreement

By using the Doctor Who Dex Discord bot, you agree to follow these terms and rules. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the bot. Servers are responsible for their members, and access may be revoked if deemed necessary.

## 2. Bot Functionality

Doctor Who Dex allows you to catch, collect, trade, and battle virtual Doctor Who cards, referred to as "collectibles" in these terms. The bot's primary purpose is entertainment, and it is strictly not intended for any real-world transactions.

## 3. User Conduct

### Cheating and Unfair Advantage

* The use of third-party tools, exploits, macros, self-bots, automated user accounts, or any other method to gain an unfair advantage or automate card catching is strictly prohibited.
* Any attempts to modify or manipulate the game mechanics to bypass intended gameplay are considered cheating.

### Exploits and Bugs

* If you discover a bug or exploit (including within the trading or battle systems), you are required to report it to the Doctor Who Dex team immediately.
* Using known exploits or bugs for personal gain is prohibited and will be penalized.

### Account Sharing and Alts

* Sharing your account with others is not allowed. Each user is responsible for the actions taken on their account.
* Creating or using multiple alternative accounts ("alts") for the purpose of gaining an advantage (e.g., farming card spawns, holding multiple daily rewards) is also prohibited.

### Farming

* Abusing spawn mechanics, such as repeatedly engaging in spam or actions designed to artificially force or accelerate spawns for cards, is not allowed.
* Any behavior that disrupts the natural balance of the game mechanics to gain items faster than normal is prohibited.

### Staff Impersonation

* Impersonating Doctor Who Dex staff, moderators, developers, or administrators is strictly forbidden.
* This includes, but is not limited to, using similar usernames, profile pictures, or any other methods intended to deceive other users.

### User Behavior

* Users must treat all members of the Doctor Who Dex community with respect. Engaging in harassment, bullying, or any form of abusive behavior through bot features (such as custom card configurations or battles) is strictly prohibited.

## 4. Guidelines for Trading and Battles

### Finality of Trades and Transfers

All trades (`/trade`) and transfers are final. Once completed, they cannot be reversed except under the specific circumstances outlined below.

### Built-in Trade Safeguards

The trading system includes a lock and confirm mechanism, along with a timer, to ensure all parties have adequate time to review and verify the details of the trade. Users are entirely responsible for double-checking all trade details before confirming.

### Reversal of Trades

Trades can only be reversed under the following conditions, strictly at staff discretion:

* **Staff Impersonation:** If a user falsely claims to be staff or support and uses this coercion to manipulate a trade.
* No other scam reports or trade disputes will be honored. Users are encouraged to exercise caution and verify all details before confirming trades.

### Cross-Trading

Cross-trading refers to the act of trading Doctor Who Dex collectibles, items, or accounts for:

1. Items, currency, or digital assets that do not belong to this bot.
2. Real-world money or financial transactions.
3. Exclusive access to an external digital space or platform.
4. Entrance to real-world events or external giveaways.
5. Any item or asset outside of the Doctor Who Dex ecosystem.

Cross-trading is strictly forbidden for any user with no exceptions and will be severely punished with permanent blacklisting.

### Card decorations via Ko-fi

* **Finality of Sale:** All purchases of digital decorations through the Ko-fi shop are **final** and non-refundable.
* **No Refunds:** This policy applies regardless of whether the decoration is **purchased**, **claimed** via `/decorations claim`, or remains **unclaimed**.
* **Usage Status:** Refunds will not be issued whether the decoration is currently **applied** to a card or sitting in your **inventory**.
* **User Responsibility:** Users must provide the correct **Ko-fi order URL** or **Transaction ID**. We are not responsible for lost decorations due to shared transaction details or user error.
* **Account Compliance:** Access to purchased decorations requires adherence to bot rules. If an account is **blacklisted** for bot abuse (e.g., farming), all decorations are **forfeited** without a refund.
